AWS Cloud Computing:
IaaS, PaaS, SaaS.

IaaS:
deploy services such as S3, EC2
customers are responsible for OS, configs, apps, patching
Amazon is responsible for the underlying hardware and make them run.

PaaS:
Customers deploys managed platform.
Customer is responsible for limited config settings and all data.
Amazon does the EC2 insts and underlying infra and services.

SaaS:
Customer deploys or deploys high level software
Customer is responsible for limited config settings + all data
Amazon manages the underlying VMs, software, and infra.

SLA, service level agreements. Service credits, Billing, Dates of Updates.
